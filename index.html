The Get Dressed Club

The Get Dressed Club a self care fullstack CRUD app for friends to take care of themselves and eachother during quarantine. Users can get outfit themes for the day, submit daily logs of their self care routine, and offer or ask for support if they are struggling. Built with React.js, Node, Postgres, Knex, & Formik. The app is designed to be reminisent of early 2000s internet to give users a nostalgic feel and remind them of when we were kids and did not have such big worries.
